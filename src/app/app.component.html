Bug reproduction

  1. Run this app on Linux.
  2. Click "Open" to open the entered URL. A new WebviewWindow will be opened with the URL. This should work.
  3. Click "Break it!". 10 WebviewWindows will be opened and immediately closed. The last one will kept open. It will be blank instead of showing a website.
  4. Click "Open" again. A new WebviewWindow will be opened but the window is blank.
